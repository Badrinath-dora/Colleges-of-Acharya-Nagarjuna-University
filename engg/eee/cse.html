About Department

Welcome to CSE Department

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the Acharya Nagarjuna University, was established in the year 2009 with anintake of 60 students in first year and intake is extended to 66 in 2021. The department has also started B.tech(Cyber Security, Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, Data Science) in the year of 2022 with an intake of 66, M.Tech( CSE-Digital Image Processing) program in the year 2011 with an intake of 18 and enrolled Ph.D students in 2010.
