DEPARTMENT PROFILE

Welcome to Electrical & Electronics Engineering Department

The EEE department was established in the year 2009. The department strives for excellence in teaching research consultancy services to the industry. It has eminence in selected niche areas of Electrical and Electronics Engineering. The Department has highly qualified and experienced faculty with a strong research and professional expertise. Besides teaching undergraduate and post-graduate courses, the faculty members are engaged in a broad range of research and development areas in collaboration with renowned overseas universities, research institutions and multinational companies. The department offering a four year B. Tech Program in EEE, a two year M. Tech program in Power systems engineering and PhD programin Electrical Engineering.
